Many Communities, One Water

Recycled Water as a Tool for Integrated Resource Planning

When it comes to social and economic vitality, potable and non-potable water is a critical resource. That’s why MWH — now part of Stantec — wants to get more out of every single drop. Communities across the globe increasingly look for profound new approaches to providing clean, potable water; we lead the way when it comes to helping clients incorporate wastewater recycling into broader planning initiatives, all while preserving natural waterbodies and improving resiliency to climate change. Join our clients near and far in a move toward greater implementation of water reuse.
